About Us

Arohan Infocare Pvt. Ltd. (An ISO 9001:2015 Company) is a leading consulting firm committed to delivering innovative solutions, specializing in Advanced Traffic Management Systems (ATMS) and Toll Management Systems (TMS). We pride ourselves on our ability to integrate our own technologies, providing customized solutions that combine the best technological capacities of the domain as well as open systems with extensive experience.

In the realm of Advanced Traffic Management Systems (ATMS) , our expertise covers the full spectrum of project requirements, from initial consultation and system design to the implementation of advanced traffic control mechanisms. Our ATMS solutions are scalable, secure, and tailored to meet the unique demands of each client, resulting in improved traffic flow and reduced congestion. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies and best practices, Arohan Infocare Pvt. Ltd. is dedicated to advancing the future of intelligent transportation systems and contributing to safer, more efficient road networks.

For Toll Management Systems (TMS), we specialize in automated toll collection processes that enhance revenue management for toll operators. We provide customized solutions that address the unique requirements of each client, ensuring scalability and security. Our TMS services include system design, development, installation, and maintenance, all aimed at improving operational efficiency and reducing congestion at toll points. With our extensive experience and innovative technologies, Arohan Infocare Pvt. Ltd. is committed to delivering superior TMS solutions that drive success for our clients.
