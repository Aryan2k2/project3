OFC Networking

We have a young and energetic team with vast experience in Optical fibre Cable networking and expertise in managing end-to-end activities from designing, planning, executing, testing, and maintenance.

Route Survey
Using high quality GP devices, Distance measuring tools, and Drawing material for accurate information.
Trenching & Ducting techniques
We are specialized in every related techniques be it Mini Trenching that is used in routes that contain asphalt surfaces such as sidewalks and roads or be it Micro Trenching that is used in routes where we require to create shallow trench.
We used standard practices to lay conduits, draw inner ducts through the conduits and finally blow the fiber through ducts using specialized fiber blowers.
Aerial Cabling
We provide services in types of Aerial cabling be it Catenary wiring or Self-Supporting or All-Dielectric Self Supporting (ADSS) cabling.
Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD)
We are also specialized in trenchless techniques such as HDD. We use every specialized tool such as MWD sensors, Mud Motors, BHA configurations, Drill Bits and Whip stock in carry out activities.
Splicing
We used international standard splicing technology like Fusion methods to carry out our activities. And also Mechanical splicing based on industry and its one time and recurring investment. We follow every step of splicing as Preparing the fibre, Cleave the Fibre, Fuse the Fibre/Mechanically join the Fibre and Protect the Fibre.
We have also worked on Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire (OPGW) that is used for installation on new power lines with double function of ground wire and communication, especially for installation on normal voltage and extra high voltage power lines.
End to End Link Testing
We used specialized test instrument like OTDR (Optical Time Domain Reflectometer) for Point to point testing.
We conduct Light source & Optical Power Meter Test that is useful for checking the optical power in performing link-loss testing or trouble-shooting.
We conduct Polarization mode dispersion test (PMD) for single mode fibers.
We conduct Chromatic Dispersion Test (CD Test) for different wave lengths of light pulses.
Final Link Test to establish the connectivity from one end to the other end of a particular stretch.
TMaintenance Activities
Maintenance of OFC routes 24*7 for Routine OFC Maintenance and Breakdown OFC Maintenance.
Preventive and Corrective Maintenance is carried out as defined by the SLA / SOW.
Any defects in the fibre or in any of the associated equipment is attended to, in consultation with the Customer at site.
Test link attributes like the splice loss/link loss after the rectification of fault are corrected.
O&M team has a supervisor, splicer, assistant splicer, patroller and a helper for every range defined by customer.
