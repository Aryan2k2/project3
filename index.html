Welcome To Arohan

Arohan Infocare Pvt. Ltd. (An ISO 9001:2015 Company) is a leading consulting firm committed to delivering innovative solutions, specializing in Advanced Traffic Management System (ATMS) and Toll Management Systems (TMS). We pride ourselves on our ability to integrate our own technologies, providing customized solutions that combine the best technological capacities of the domain as well as open systems with extensive experience. From installation, operation, and maintenance, we ensure every stage of an ATMS and TMS project is executed with meticulous attention to detail. Our skilled team works collaboratively with clients to navigate complexities, mitigate risks, and achieve their business objectives, providing end-to-end services from design and development to testing and integration.

In the realm of ATMS and TMS , our expertise covers the full spectrum of project requirements, from initial consultation and system design to the implementation of advanced traffic control mechanisms and automated toll collection processes. We ensure that our solutions are scalable, secure, and tailored to meet the unique demands of each client, resulting in improved traffic flow, reduced congestion, and enhanced revenue management for toll operators. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies and best practices, Arohan Infocare Pvt. Ltd. is dedicated to advancing the future of intelligent transportation systems and contributing to safer, more efficient road networks.
