Toll Management System(TMS)

Welcome to Arohan Infocare's Toll Management Service (TMS), a state-of-the-art system designed to revolutionize toll collection and traffic management. Our advanced TMS integrates cutting-edge technology and sophisticated software to ensure efficient, secure, and seamless toll collection.

What is Toll Management System (TMS)?
Toll Management System (TMS) is designed to collect revenue and recover capital outlay incurred on construction, maintenance, and operation of toll facilities. This system aims to provide reduced travel time and enhanced service levels to end-users. Arohan Infocare’s TMS is a blend of various hardware and software modules that automate and manage the entire toll collection process efficiently.

Key Features of Arohan Infocare’s TMS:
Automated Toll Collection
- Facilitates smooth traffic flow
- Minimizes leakages and increases revenues
- Handles over 100,000+ PCU per day

Fraud Detection and Incident Management
- Detects fraud and incidents
- Eases operations and minimizes risk

Automated Vehicle Classification (AVC)
 - Determines exact toll charge

Advanced Data Processing
 - Facilitates faster and accurate processing of vehicular data via Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR)
- Works in conjunction with Speed Enforcement engines

Manual Toll Collection

Semi-Automatic Toll Collection
- Traditional toll collection with human operators

Our Premier Solution
Our flagship product, exemplifies the integration of advanced technologies to create an intelligent traffic management system. Key Features of Arohan Infocare’s ATMS:
Automatic Traffic Counter & Classifier (ATCC):
 This technology detects, classifies, and estimates vehicle speeds across multiple lanes and directions, providing accurate and reliable traffic data.

Video Incident Detection System (VIDS):
Offers comprehensive visual coverage, enabling real-time detection and recording of traffic incidents.

Closed Circuit Television Surveillance (CCTV):
 Ensures continuous monitoring, even under low visibility conditions.

Variable Message Sign (VMS):
Communicates critical information such as weather updates, traffic congestion, and road diversions directly to drivers.

Solar Blinkers:
Eco-friendly solar-powered blinkers to regulate traffic on less congested roads and highways.
